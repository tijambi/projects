  • True leader influences others to be their best. Leadership is about social influence, not positional power. If you're a slave to the status quo, lack vision, or don't motivate everyone around you to be their absolute best, then you're a follower. Even if you happen to have a leadership title, people won't follow you when they see those behaviors present. You're not a leader just because you have people reporting to you. And you don't suddenly become a leader once you reach a certain pay grade.

    If your actions inspire others to dream more, learn more, do more and become more, you are a leader. You don't even need to have people reporting to you to be a leader. A janitor can influence people and lead just as a CEO can. Likewise, anyone can become a follower, even while holding a leadership position
